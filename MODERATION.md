# Fæsh Community & Moderation Guidelines

## Our Values
Fæsh is inclusive, creative, and welcoming to all cultures and identities.

## Prohibited Content
- Harassment or hate speech
- Exploitation or abuse
- Fraud or impersonation
- Explicit content involving minors

## Enforcement
Content may be removed and accounts restricted to protect the community.

## Reporting
Users can report content directly within the platform.
